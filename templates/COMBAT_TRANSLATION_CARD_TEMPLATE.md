## Applicability

Use this template when combat, pursuit, physical threat, hostile magic, meaningful gear use under threat, consequential power use, injury, evasion, no-hit result, damage result or tactical prediction occurs.

If none of those occurs, write `combat pre-check: not applicable` in the Chapter Loadout and give one sentence of reason. Do not fill this template merely to make a quiet scene look tactical.

## Authority and obligation placement

- This card applies source, Canon Delta, chapter plan, mini delta and scene cards to the threat sequence.
- It may close Level 1–3 execution details required for causal safety: board, physical lanes, no-hit causes, damage ownership, capabilities used/prohibited and aftermath.
- It may define Level 4 tactical targets such as pressure rhythm, preferred staging and clarity.
- Level 5 prose order, exact motion wording and non-outcome texture remain free.
- It must not create a source-outcome change, casualty count, power permission, kill ownership, legal conclusion, relationship consequence or required ally action that lacks higher authority.
- Repetition across combat artifacts does not raise authority.
- If combat logic requires a higher-authority change, return it to the Canon Delta or other owning artifact before drafting.
