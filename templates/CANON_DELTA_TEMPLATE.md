# C2EX Canon Delta

## Authority placement

This is the owner for episode-level and chapter-level adaptation decisions that change, preserve under pressure, or explicitly certify source outcomes.

Record here before chapter-specific files apply the decision:

- source outcome changes or certified preservation under Mielle's presence;
- casualty count, injury count, capture/escape/detainment result or legal/faction outcome;
- ownership of deaths, decisive hits, rescues, finalizations and source accomplishments;
- power availability, power prohibition, gear state or deliberate non-use that can affect an outcome;
- relationship-state implications or explicit non-implications created by the adaptation;
- witness facts that materially affect later continuity.

Chapter plans, mini deltas, scene cards and combat pre-checks apply this file to execution. They do not replace it.
