## Boundary receipt

- This Arc Map may define cross-episode causality, delayed revelations, agency ownership and entry/exit state.
- It may not decide chapter divisions, final dialogue, scene choreography, exact power use or a canon change.
- A conflict with approved continuity or stable canon blocks the map and returns to the owning source.
