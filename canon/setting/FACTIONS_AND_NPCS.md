# Factions and NPCs

## Dwendalian Empire

The Dwendalian Empire governs Western Wynandir under King Bertrand Dwendal.

## Crownsguard

The Crownsguard is the Empire’s main law-enforcement body. Trostenwald has a local contingent.

## Cobalt Soul

The Cobalt Soul is a scholarly religious order associated with Ioun. It preserves and shares knowledge, and has an archive in Zadash.

## The Traveler

The Traveler is the figure worshiped by Jester. His common symbol is an arched doorway over a road vanishing into the distance. His worship is not approved by the Empire.

## Carnival

At C2E1, the Fletching and Moondrop Traveling Carnival of Curiosities is operating in Trostenwald.

## Venue staff

Yorda manages the Nestled Nook. Adelaine works there. Rinaldo is a local fisherman in the C2E1 source.
