# Locations

## Scope

This file records durable geographic and venue facts. Exact scene staging belongs in transcript extraction, episode maps and scene cards.

## Marrow Valley and Amber Road

C2E1 begins in the southern Marrow Valley of Western Wynandir. The Amber Road is a major imperial road linking the Wuyun Gates, Zadash, Alfield, Trostenwald and Rexxentrum.

Use the road as a source of travelers, freight and temporary work. Do not invent distances, schedules, tolls or local conditions without a source.

## Trostenwald

Trostenwald is a small rural trade town in the Marrow Valley. The Ustaloch lies to its east and the Amber Road to its west. It has three wards: Lochward, Hillward and Northward.

The town’s economy rests on fish, crops and local brewing. A Crownsguard contingent provides law enforcement.

The wiki gives conflicting or time-sensitive names and titles for the town’s civil authority. Do not name a C2E1 civic leader unless the episode source establishes that person.

## The Nestled Nook Inn

The Nestled Nook is a two-story inn in Trostenwald. It provides food and lodging.

- Yorda manages the inn.
- Adelaine works as a barmaid.
- The upper floor has three rooms and a communal washroom at the end of the hall.
- The upper hall has no windows; the rooms do.
- A job board is mounted on a wall.

## Ustaloch and carnival grounds

Lochward contains Trostenwald’s waterside docks and fishing activity. At C2E1, the Fletching and Moondrop Traveling Carnival of Curiosities has set up east of Lochward on the Ustaloch’s south bank.

The exact carnival layout belongs to episode work.
