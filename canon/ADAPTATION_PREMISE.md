# Adaptation premise

## Starting point

The story begins with *Critical Role* Campaign 2, Episode 1.

## Mielle’s position

Mielle arrives in the area near Trostenwald two days before the episode opening. She does not know Exandria or its local history. She has a partial return of sealed memory and power, hunger, a temporary abandoned shelter, and no established connection to the Mighty Nein.

## First contact boundary

Mielle first encounters the group at the Episode 1 tavern. Jester offers food after noticing Mielle’s condition. Mielle does not join the group immediately; she returns to her shelter. Molly later invites her to the carnival, creating a second meeting.

The exact staging, dialogue, seating, sequence of reactions and whether anyone notices the bokken remain undecided until transcript extraction, Episode Map, Canon Delta and scene cards.

## Adaptation boundary

The transcript controls original outcomes, causal choices, information flow and later consequences unless an approved Canon Delta records a change. Mielle may alter pressure, options, observation, protection, complications and emotional consequences. She may not silently replace a decision or accomplishment that must remain with a canon character.
