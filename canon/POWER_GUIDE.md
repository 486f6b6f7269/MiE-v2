# Mielle power guide

## Scope

This file controls the v2 starting interpretation of Mielle’s power access. It does not recreate the later continuity, visibility tiers, recovery restrictions or power-state locks from the former repository.

## Pre-seal source record

The historical inventory source is `reference/MIELLE_ENCYCLOPEDIA_v4.md` in `486f6b6f7269/Mielle-in-Exandria-`. The former current-guide modules are reference for source mechanics only, not for their later-story access classifications.

## C2E1 access principle

When Mielle transmigrates, the partial break in her seal returns practical access to the capabilities associated with the memories she recovers. Do not import v1 restrictions, tiers, recovery percentages, or visibility locks by default. Any new limit must come from an explicit user decision, an approved chapter outcome, or a source mechanic that is part of the named capability itself.

## Equipment boundary

Nearly all pre-seal equipment remains inaccessible in Mielle’s pocket dimension. At C2E1, she has practical access to Perfect Wardrobe, Gale-Force Glasses and one ordinary soulbound wooden bokken. The Bag of Holding is used through a backpack concealment method.

## Use method

Before a named capability is used in prose:

1. identify the legacy source description;
2. state what Mielle is trying to do in the present scene;
3. apply the capability’s actual described mechanics;
4. preserve other characters’ agency and the transcript’s required causal beats;
5. record any new stable interpretation only after chapter approval.

## No automatic restrictions

This guide deliberately does not carry forward the former project’s power tiers, present-state classifications, recovery throttles or user-directed access protocol. Those are not v2 canon unless added through a later explicit decision.
