# I. Current named inventory — entries 11–20

## 11. Soulbond

- **Source:** Spiritual and Divine Powers (Original) — `9pta`.
- **Core effect:** Binds one nonliving item to Mielle’s soul. A bound item can be stored and recalled at will, located remotely, inherit perks and a fraction of passive powers, and become indestructible unless Mielle wills otherwise.
- **Expression:** voluntary binding, retraction, storage and recall.
- **Scope:** the source gives global location sense unless protected; no recall travel time is specified.
- **C2E1 core gear:** the bokken, Gale-Force Reading Glasses and Perfect Wardrobe are soulbound.
- **Source limits:** Soulbond cannot bind living creatures. It does not force-bind another person’s equipment, create items, bypass every anti-soul or anti-dimensional defense, or make any item appropriate to retrieve in a scene. Consumables and perishables recover over time; stronger items take longer.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Multi-Bond requires Soulbond. Relevant links include the Bag of Holding and all accessible core gear.

Conqueror’s Haki must never be used as a shortcut to consent, loyalty, confession, romance or agency removal.
