# I. Current named inventory — entries 1–10

## 1. Thousand Styles Master

- **Source:** Skills — `u4x5`.
- **Core effect:** Mastery of free-form martial arts. Mielle can move between unarmed work, staff or paired-stick work, weapon handling, movement, and improvised positional answers without treating each as a separate discipline.
- **Expression:** trained, embodied skill; no explicit activation.
- **Scope:** personal, direct physical action.
- **Source limits:** This entry does not itself grant superhuman speed, durability, anatomical knowledge, predictive certainty, immunity to fatigue, or automatic adaptation to unknown supernatural physiology.
- **Prerequisites and links:** no prerequisite. Relevant links include Black Bat, Greatest Martial Artist, Ashina Arts, Sword Saint, Flowing River, Haki, Sendo, The Spin, and the bokken.
- **Source gap:** Martial Improvisation Art was an unselected child in the former source package and is not inferred from this entry.

## 2. Invictus

- **Source:** Perks — `5r67`.
- **Core effect:** Exceptional will, patience, endurance of purpose, composure under extreme hardship, and a durable sense of self.
- **Expression:** passive and continuous.
- **Scope:** self only.
- **Source limits:** Invictus does not erase depression, anxiety, grief, pain, fear, exhaustion, uncertainty, or the possibility of being hurt. It does not make Mielle automatically correct. The source warns that endurance can become tunnel vision; she must still recognize other people’s agency.
- **Prerequisites and links:** no prerequisite. Relevant links include Dream for the Stars, Haki, Will of D., Flowing River, Theoretical Best Move, and the mental-resistance architecture.

## 3. Bag of Holding

- **Source:** Tier 1 Shaker — `ycv8`.
- **Core effect:** Projects a portal into a pocket dimension with apparently immense storage. Mielle knows its contents and can retrieve a chosen stored item. The aperture can enlarge to accept a larger object.
- **Expression:** projects a portal; may telepathically order the portal to eject an item.
- **Scope:** the source gives no portal range or persistence duration. Ejected objects travel at 60 mph or less.
- **C2E1 accessible contents:** the bokken, Gale-Force Reading Glasses, and Perfect Wardrobe only.
- **Source limits:** The Bag does not generate contents, money, food, maps, shelter, tools, ammunition, or equipment. The source does not establish safe storage for living beings, perfect concealment of the portal, automatic passage through anti-dimensional barriers, battlefield artillery, or a safe exit route for the party.
- **Presentation:** Mielle may retrieve an accessible item directly into her hand or nearby air when concealment is irrelevant. She may use the backpack as an ordinary-looking gesture when discretion is kinder or safer. Direct retrieval remains visible and extraordinary.
- **Prerequisites and links:** no prerequisite. Relevant links include Soulbond, Multi-Bond, and core-gear concealment.

## 4. Ares, Legion of One

- **Source:** Martial Arts and Techniques (Original) — `ti6lsr`.
- **Core effect:** An inherited archive of soldiers’ memories and learned capacities across human and alternate-human conflicts, including combat, administration, civilian survival, logistics, strategy and practical fieldcraft.
- **Expression:** Mielle can call upon the archive; it is not a separate spell or mode.
- **Scope:** cognitive archive; no range or duration.
- **Source limits:** The archive carries mental contamination from experiencing the collected lives. “Soldier” includes conscripts, insurgents, rebels and people fighting for or against a country. It does not supply perfect local Exandrian knowledge, personal ownership of archived deeds, moral certainty, or permission to replace party agency with command.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Steel, Mud, and Faith requires Ares. Relevant links include Holmes, Theoretical Best Move, medical practice, logistics, tactics and civilian survival.

## 5. Ashina Arts

- **Source:** Martial Arts and Techniques (Franchise) — `unjp`.
- **Core effect:** Ashina sword training built around fluid transitions between offense, defense and counterattack; includes archery. The source benchmark compares its sword skill to Genichiro’s.
- **Expression:** trained skill.
- **Scope:** personal weapon range; no fixed numerical values.
- **Source limits:** This entry does not grant Genichiro’s supernatural abilities, armor, lightning, immortality, equipment or setting privileges. It does not guarantee victory in a duel, supply supernatural posture damage, or provide a free lightning reversal.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Sword Saint requires Ashina Arts. Listed but unselected children include Way of Tomoe, Shadow Dance and Sword of Messmer. Relevant links include Thousand Styles Master, Sword Saint, Flowing River, Haki, Black Bat, bow use and bokken use.

## 6. Holmes

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Powers (Original) — `759c`.
- **Core effect:** Greatly improves perception and deduction. Conclusions require evidence and are less error-prone than broad inference systems that guess from sparse data.
- **Expression:** passive attention and deliberate reasoning.
- **Scope:** limited by available evidence and Mielle’s ordinary or enhanced senses; no numerical range.
- **Source limits:** Holmes does not improve guessing. It does not create missing facts, read private thoughts, supply local lore that Mielle has not learned, or authorize intrusion into private matters. A decisive anchor or very small high-information clue set may support a near-certain narrow conclusion, but genuine unseen branches remain unknown until tested or otherwise resolved.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Sherlock Scan and Moriarty require Holmes. Simulated World was a listed child but is not inferred. Relevant links include Black Bat, Observation Haki, Gale-Force Reading Glasses, Sherlock Scan, Mycroft and Theoretical Best Move.
- **Scene controller:** use `canon/HOLMES_ENGINE.md` for scene architecture, high-confidence deduction, privacy, correction and agency release.

## 7. Dream for the Stars

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Powers (Franchise) — `DreamfortheStars`.
- **Core effect:** Maintains objective thought, clarifies a reason to exist, preserves personally held dreams, reduces fear of early or natural death, and can help people trapped in resignation imagine a better future.
- **Expression:** passive presence; interpersonal effect occurs through conversation, example and relationship rather than stated mind control.
- **Scope:** relational and contextual; no numerical range or duration.
- **Source limits:** It does not compel hope, cure depression, make dreams attainable, impose a purpose, or create charisma domination.
- **Prerequisites and links:** The Light of Sirius was an unselected child. Relevant links include Invictus, Sagacious Wisdom and recovery or moral-support scenes.

## 8. Sagacious Wisdom

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Powers (Franchise) — `rufi2v`.
- **Core effect:** High-order wisdom and insight. It can inspect Mielle’s own thought patterns, help pierce illusions or expose lies and mysteries, identify non-consensual mind control or undue influence including social manipulation, drugs and parasites, and support cross-pollination between art, science and engineering.
- **Expression:** observation and reflection.
- **Scope:** no stated range or duration.
- **Source limits:** “Lies reveal themselves” is source language, not a universal truth guarantee or a substitute for corroboration. It does not create total omniscience, automatic knowledge of any magical system, or a right to scan another person’s mind.
- **Prerequisites and links:** none listed. Relevant links include Holmes, Mycroft, Invictus, Raphael, Polymath and the protection architecture.

## 9. Black Bat

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Powers (Franchise) — `BlackBat`.
- **Core effect:** Cassandra Cain-style fluency with readable body language. It supports combat, stealth, acrobatics and nonverbal communication.
- **Expression:** requires an observable body and meaningful movement.
- **Scope:** no numerical range; it works through readable physical behavior.
- **Source limits:** The source states that it works only on humans, that truly unpredictable actions can become gibberish, and that its death-perception effect arises if Mielle kills someone. It does not supply anatomy knowledge for a new species, precise prediction in poor visibility, moral knowledge or perfect counters against alien or nonhuman movement.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Greatest Martial Artist, Multilingual, Third Eye and Erratic Predictions are listed children; only Greatest Martial Artist belongs to this current package. Relevant links include Thousand Styles Master, Ashina Arts, Greatest Martial Artist, Observation Haki and Holmes.

## 10. Greatest Martial Artist

- **Source:** Black Bat child — `durww01p`.
- **Core effect:** Adds Cassandra Cain’s accumulated martial experience and every martial art that existed on DC Earth during her life.
- **Expression:** trained and embodied skill.
- **Scope:** personal combat.
- **Source limits:** It inherits Black Bat’s human-body-language limitation. It does not grant physical stat enhancement, alien anatomy knowledge or free use of every external martial system’s supernatural energy.
- **Prerequisites and links:** requires Thousand Styles Master in the source record. Relevant links include Thousand Styles Master, Black Bat, Ashina Arts, Haki, Sendo and Sword Saint.
