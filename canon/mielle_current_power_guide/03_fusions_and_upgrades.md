# II. Fusions and upgrades — entries 21–29

## 21. Sword Saint

- **Source:** Physical Power Upgrade — `n3em`; requires Ashina Arts.
- **Core effect:** Divine-level weapon mastery, including sword arts comparable to One Mind. Mastery transfers to other weapons. The source also grants lightning-like speed, blows capable of shattering buildings and comparable durability.
- **Expression:** trained and embodied combat capability.
- **Scope:** personal weapon range; the source explicitly includes building-shattering physical output.
- **Source limits:** Sword Saint does not grant immunity to local magic, automatic One Mind in every exchange, or an obligation to display its full physical scale in every duel. It supplies its stated mechanics, not the complete boss kit or setting privileges of Sekiro.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Ashina Arts required; Flowing River is a selected child. Relevant links include Thousand Styles Master, Haki and Black Bat.

## 22. Flowing River

- **Source:** Sword Saint child — `hkpa46wk`; requires Invictus.
- **Core effect:** Blue Dancer and Malenia-derived fluid combat: continuous motion, counters through intentional openings, delayed strike timing, Waterfowl Dance-style movement, and recovery of health and resolve when landing attacks.
- **Expression:** combat movement and successful strikes.
- **Scope:** personal melee. The source gives no numerical healing amount.
- **Source limits:** This entry does not grant Scarlet Rot, Malenia’s wings, guaranteed recovery from every glancing hit, immunity to counters or automatic recovery of any unspecified injury. Treat health recovery as the effect actually established by the scene, not as a quantified system the source does not provide.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Sword Saint plus Invictus. Relevant links include Ashina Arts, Haki and Thousand Styles Master.

## 23. Steel, Mud, and Faith

- **Source:** Physical Power Upgrade — `0kuc8v`; requires Ares, Legion of One.
- **Core effect:** Expands the Ares archive with Astra Militarum experience: siege, mechanized, guerrilla, ash-waste, hostile-environment and anti-horror warfare. It allows Mielle to compare and use distinct military doctrines.
- **Expression:** retrieval and application of archival experience.
- **Scope:** cognitive; no range or duration.
- **Source limits:** It deepens Ares’s mental contamination and carries contradictory courage, despair, discipline, faith, shame and fatalism. It does not provide Astra Militarum equipment, local military authority, personal war biography or permission to import 40k political or religious assumptions into Exandria.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Ares required. Relevant links include Holmes, Theoretical Best Move and logistics or field support.

## 24. Sherlock Scan

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Upgrade — `m8jz`; requires Holmes.
- **Core effect:** Visualizes engagements from beginning to end and supports dozens-of-steps-ahead planning in fights or games. It also improves intelligence, processing, multitasking and senses.
- **Expression:** deliberate plan construction from evidence.
- **Scope:** no numerical planning horizon, range or duration.
- **Source limits:** It is only as accurate as the conditions and inputs it can model. New information, hidden variables, unknown magic, moral choices, chaotic people and independent allies or enemies can invalidate a plan. It does not grant a guaranteed future, omniscience or control over participants.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Holmes required; Mycroft upgrades it. Relevant links include Black Bat, Observation Haki, Theoretical Best Move and Ares.

## 25. Mycroft

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Upgrade — `jmzed4`; requires Sherlock Scan.
- **Core effect:** Vastly stronger deduction and analysis than Sherlock Scan; exceptional clue extraction; strong resistance to mind probes from peers and meaningful resistance to manipulation from a superior intellect such as Eurus.
- **Expression:** observation and analysis; mental resistance is passive or reactive.
- **Scope:** no numerical range, duration or threshold.
- **Source limits:** “Practically immune” in the source refers to comparison with a Sherlock-level probe. It is not absolute immunity to every mystical, divine, physical, chemical or narrative influence. It does not create omniscient deduction or read all secrets from one glance regardless of evidence.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Sherlock Scan required. Eurus is a selected child. Relevant links include Holmes, Sagacious Wisdom, Mental Barrier, Invictus and Raphael.

## 26. Eurus

- **Source:** Mycroft child — `eebvl9so`; requires Moriarty.
- **Core effect:** Combines Sherlock, Moriarty and Mycroft into extreme intelligence and predictive reach. The source describes future-like inference and large-scale social manipulation.
- **Expression:** cognitive.
- **Scope:** no numerical range, duration or social scale is supplied.
- **Ethical boundary:** Eurus is never a shortcut to compromise free will, manufacture loyalty, force confession, create attraction, manipulate intimacy, remove consent or make another person’s choice belong to Mielle. The source does not provide a consent-safe boundary by itself; the project’s ethics do.
- **Source limits:** It does not create moral authority, a consent mechanism or a consequence-free ability to rewrite another person’s agency.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Mycroft and Moriarty required. Relevant links include the broader cognition architecture under the ethical boundary above.

## 27. Moriarty

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Upgrade — `7ha5fe`; requires Holmes.
- **Core effect:** Criminal-system knowledge, familiarity with forgery, blackmail and trafficking, planning instinct, and the capacity to establish or manage clandestine organizations.
- **Expression:** analytical and practical skill.
- **Scope:** no numerical scale; any “world-spanning network” language is capacity, not an existing organization.
- **Ethical boundary:** Use this knowledge to identify covert structures, recognize sabotage, trace hostile systems and dismantle threats. It is not a reason for Mielle to build a criminal empire or use criminal tactics because they are available.
- **Source limits:** It does not grant lawful authority, infallible criminal detection or moral permission for coercive methods.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Holmes required; Eurus requires Moriarty. Relevant links include Sherlock Scan, Mycroft, Ares and Theoretical Best Move.

## 28. Theoretical Best Move

- **Source:** Mental and Psychic Upgrade — `io8573`; requires Holmes.
- **Core effect:** Combines the mental acuity, intelligence, memory, creativity, willpower, charisma, game theory, strategy, languages, media knowledge, cold reading, predictive ability, reflexes, skills, luck and game sense of both members of `[ ]`.
- **Expression:** formulate an objective and evaluate available information and constraints.
- **Scope:** no fixed range, duration or output. It does not choose a morally acceptable objective for Mielle.
- **Source limits:** Unknown variables, local metaphysics, independent choices, incompatible values and ethical boundaries remain real. It does not provide absolute precognition, compel an opponent into a game, bypass consent or create a right answer where goals conflict morally.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Holmes required. Relevant links include Holmes, Sherlock Scan, Mycroft, Ares, Sagacious Wisdom and Invictus.

## 29. Multi-Bond

- **Source:** Spiritual and Divine Upgrade — `zb30`; requires Soulbond.
- **Core effect:** Extends Soulbond from one item to hundreds or thousands. Capacity can grow through training body, mind and power or slowly over time. Bound items gain a fraction of all powers, not only passive or self-boosting ones.
- **Expression:** voluntary binding and retraction per nonliving item.
- **Scope:** no remote-recall range beyond Soulbond’s location sensing. The source describes hundreds or thousands of bonds for a baseline human.
- **Source limits:** Multi-Bond does not create items, force-bind equipment, supply unlimited independent weapons or establish which transferred abilities are meaningful for a given object. The object must exist, be nonliving and be actually available to bind or retrieve.
- **C2E1 equipment fact:** the three accessible core items are already soulbound. Other pocket-dimension equipment remains physically inaccessible even though Multi-Bond exists.
- **Prerequisites and links:** Soulbond required. Relevant links include Perfect Wardrobe, Gale-Force Reading Glasses, the bokken and Bag of Holding.
