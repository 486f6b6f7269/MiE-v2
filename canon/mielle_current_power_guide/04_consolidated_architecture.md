# III. Consolidated architecture and scene use

## Purpose

This module groups selected entries that work together around mind, identity, body, power integrity, time, cognition, memory and scene execution.

It is a capability map. It does not invent a new technique, range, output or manifestation beyond the listed source entries.

## A. Mind, senses, agency and memetic threats

### Covered entries

- **Mental Barrier (`42jg`):** blocks direct effects on mind, emotions and senses; covers perception filters, hypnotic tinker-tech, body control or possession, and memetic hazards. It permits consent.
- **Blood-Brain Barrier (`xf39pr81`):** closes the stated gap involving drugs, pheromones and parasites.
- **Invictus (`5r67`):** preserves will, purpose, composure and selfhood under trauma or endurance pressure; it does not make Mielle emotionless.
- **Sagacious Wisdom (`rufi2v`):** can inspect Mielle’s thought patterns and identify non-consensual mind control or undue influence, including social manipulation, drugs and parasites.
- **Mycroft (`jmzed4`):** provides strong resistance to a Sherlock-level mind probe and meaningful resistance to superior Eurus-level manipulation, without becoming universal immunity.
- **I Don’t Get It (`whoknows`) and I Get It Now… But Not Really…? (`3laexxaz`):** prevent sanity-breaking or eldritch cognitive reactions from destabilizing Mielle. They are not truth detectors.
- **Black Bat and Holmes:** read observable movement, evidence and inconsistency, not private thoughts.

### Practical result

Hostile attempts to hypnotize, mentally overwrite, possess, force sensory falsehoods, infect Mielle with a memetic hazard or socially steer her without her awareness meet a layered defense. A useful scene sequence is:

> reject → detect or analyze → choose a response.

### Boundaries

- A mental defense does not prove a person is lying, reveal a motive or make a social interpretation correct.
- Consent remains meaningful. A defense may be selectively permitted where the underlying entry allows it.
- Physical effects still matter unless a specific entry addresses them. Mielle may be physically drugged, injured or restrained only to the extent the relevant protections and scene facts allow; do not assume either total immunity or total vulnerability without reading the actual entry.

## B. Power integrity and anti-override

### Covered entries

- **Trumped the Trump (`w0ll`):** Mielle’s powers and internal supplies cannot be drained, suppressed, weakened, stolen, copied, altered, controlled, reproduced or manipulated against her will. She may block aspects of a power acting on her.
- **No Strings Attached (`xhdc`):** powers may operate independently of mind, body and soul; external-source powers can remain connected to the true self and function across nonstandard states.
- **Only a Sith Deals in Absolutes (`3vpf`):** gives a way to decide clashes between absolute effects and may enforce Mielle’s effects as absolute.
- **Natural (`ehfi8j`):** perfect instinctive control and understanding of all powers.

### Practical result

A hostile power does not casually turn off, steal, rewrite or hijack Mielle’s inventory. A scene claiming it does must identify a mechanism that actually reaches around these entries rather than treating suppression as generic drama.

### Boundaries

- “Cannot be manipulated against her will” does not erase voluntary choice, agreed exchange, physical injury or unknown local conditions.
- Only a Sith Deals in Absolutes requires an actual clash of absolute effects. It is not a generic answer to strong magic.
- Natural does not replace knowledge of an undefined source mechanic; perfect control is not a fabricated description.

## C. Body, soul, identity and continuity

### Covered entries

- **Soulbond (`9pta`) and Multi-Bond (`zb30`):** tie nonliving objects to Mielle’s soul for recall, location and transferred properties.
- **Continuity of Existence (`49k35s`):** Mielle remains herself through cloning, upload, reconstruction, rebirth or revival; identity, soul and powers continue rather than being replaced by a copy.
- **Humanity (`axze`):** preserves chosen human aspects while retaining alternative-race benefits.
- **Manton Protected (`idmd`):** protects the inside of the body against targeting and adds partial resistance to instant death or erasure.
- **Equilibrium (`absmrt`):** supports mind, body and soul integrity; use its exact mechanics rather than calling it generic healing.
- **Foreigner (`Naturalized_Foreigner`):** can exempt Mielle from a world’s inherent concepts, rules or restrictions while allowing desired benefits.

### Practical result

Mielle’s identity is hard to replace, forcibly separate or internally overwrite. Her core bound objects are correspondingly difficult to remove or destroy.

### Boundaries

- Continuity of Existence does not create an automatic resurrection scene without a mechanism.
- Soul protection does not make the body invulnerable to every physical attack.
- No entry permits binding a living person or treating another person’s soul as equipment.
- Manton Protected addresses invasive internal targeting. It does not automatically stop external heat, cold, impact, pressure, terrain, binding, poison delivered through lawful routes, or every environmental danger.
- Foreigner does not become a generic “setting immunity” phrase. Identify the imposed rule, the desired exemption and the stated interaction before using it.

## D. Chronal, retroactive, narrative and metaphysical interference

### Covered entries

- **Ripple Effect Proof Memory (`TimeRipple`):** retains old and revised timeline memories separately and may emotionally distance from or reconnect them by choice.
- **Ripple Effect Proof Body (`brt2s6wf`):** can reject retroactive bodily changes with scaling tied to the relative power of the timeline-altering source.
- **Blank (`uh4g`):** Thinker blindspot. It does not block direct observation or reasoning from observed evidence.
- **Reality Anchor (`03q5nt`):** local, 20-foot enforcement of experienced physical reality against foreign entities or phenomena.
- **Foreigner:** may address imposed world rules when the exact interaction is established.
- **Continuity of Existence:** preserves identity across reconstruction and revival transitions.

### Practical result

Time rewrites, history edits, predictive systems, certain reality distortions and metaphysical reclassification cannot be assumed to change Mielle cleanly or invisibly.

### Boundaries

- This is not “narrative immunity.”
- Reality Anchor is local. Read its exact effect before using it against Exandrian magic or an unfamiliar phenomenon.
- Blank hides Mielle from direct power-only observation; it does not make her invisible to people, cameras, tracks or careful reasoning.
- Ripple effects do not automatically reveal who changed time, why it happened or what consequence follows.

## E. Cognition, processing, will and creativity

### 1. Evidence and perception layer

**Holmes + Black Bat + Gale-Force Reading Glasses + Observation Haki** form Mielle’s normal information intake.

- Holmes turns evidence into bounded inference.
- Black Bat reads human movement.
- The glasses accelerate reading of visible intelligible text.
- Observation Haki supplies awareness of aura and emotion as described by its own source entry.

**Result:** earlier warning, clearer physical hypotheses, better use of visible reports and more accurate combat reading.

**Not supplied:** automatic truth, private-motive access, alien anatomy knowledge or perfect prediction inside a chaotic hidden-information fight.

### 2. Tactical and strategic layer

**Sherlock Scan + Mycroft + Theoretical Best Move + Ares + Steel, Mud, and Faith** support scenario modeling, engagement planning, logistics, doctrine comparison and route selection.

- Sherlock Scan visualizes many steps ahead when enough inputs exist.
- Mycroft improves clue extraction and resistance to hostile cognition.
- Theoretical Best Move chooses a route toward a stated objective under known conditions.
- Ares and Steel supply historical and archival precedent with psychological cost.

**Result:** Mielle can propose a precise answer and a bounded reason for it.

**Not supplied:** authority to decide the party’s goals, override choices, know hidden variables or treat a predicted route as fate.

### 3. Executive and processing layer

The broader current-guide architecture includes **Raphael, Multitask and parallel-processing entries, Polymath, Mind Palace, Noctis Cape, Practical Experience, Gale-Force Reading Glasses, Bullet Time, Organized Mind, Brain Formatting, Cognitive Language, Understanding, Swift Insight, Clarity, Problem Solving, Attention to Detail, Watchmaker, Papert’s Principle, Human Calculator, Calculations Complete, No Lag, Total Recall, Star-Nosed Mole, Lightning Fast Reflexes, Adjustable Perception and Golden Mind**.

- **Raphael:** appraisal, accelerated thought, parallel operation, analysis, synthesis, separation and alteration of non-concealed phenomena.
- **Mind Palace and Noctis Cape:** preserve and retrieve memory.
- **Polymath and supporting skills:** improve learning and cross-domain transfer.

**Result:** Mielle can monitor a field, preserve a plan, track an ally and perform ordinary work without losing the thread.

**Not supplied:** no error, no cognitive overload, no wrong values, unknown local facts, extra hands, extra bodies, extra reach, or moral decision outsourcing to Raphael.

### 4. Creativity and synthesis layer

**Sagacious Wisdom + Dream for the Stars + Polymath + artistic and technical skills + Theoretical Best Move** support grounded cross-domain ideas: art informing engineering, a tactical constraint suggesting a social answer, or a narrow tool replacing a large display of force.

**Not supplied:** spontaneous impossible invention without materials or time, prediction disguised as creativity, or coercion recast as cleverness.

### 5. Will, resilience and action layer

**Invictus + Dream for the Stars + Will of D. + Haki + Flowing River** support persistence, resolve and the ability to act through fear or pain.

**Result:** Mielle can keep a clear action line under pressure and refuse collapse merely because a situation is frightening.

**Not supplied:** fake pain, immunity to trauma, immunity to doubt or grief, or permission to ignore the physical consequence a scene has established.

## F. External-source calibration

The CYOA-derived descriptions in this package define mechanics. External franchise material may clarify a term but does not add unlisted abilities.

- **Sekiro:** Ashina and Sword Saint establish swordsmanship, archery and elite dueling context; they do not import an entire boss kit.
- **JoJo:** Hamon, Spin and Stand are distinct systems. Do not add named Stand powers, Tusk progression, Infinite Rotation or a complete JoJo cosmology without explicit definition.
- **One Piece:** Armament, Observation and Conqueror’s are distinct. Advanced variants require mechanics, not franchise-default assumption.
- **DC / Cassandra Cain:** body-language fluency applies to readable human movement; it does not convert unknown nonhuman movement into human language.
- **Gurren Lagann:** Spiral is potential driven by will and evolution. Broad potential is not a substitute for a defined scene effect.
- **Warhammer 40k:** Astra Militarum material is archive and doctrine, not rank, equipment, war or political allegiance.
- **Type-Moon and No Game No Life:** use only the mechanics actually recorded here; external lore does not expand them.

## G. Scene-use checklist

Before using any entry in prose, answer:

1. What exact entry supplies the effect?
2. What source field establishes the mechanic?
3. What trigger, target, range, duration or output is actually stated?
4. What source gap prevents the entry from becoming a shortcut?
5. Does the scene establish a physical, ethical or informational cost?
6. Does the use preserve another character’s agency and contribution?
7. Is the outcome supported by evidence and mechanics, or is the draft inventing an effect the source does not describe?

## H. Prose rule

Do not name systems such as Spiral, Rigging, Raphael, Holmes, Haki, Soulbond or Theoretical Best Move in story dialogue or narration unless an in-world term has been separately established. Show consequence, not an author-side label.
