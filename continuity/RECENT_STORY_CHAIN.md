## C2E1 Chapter 01 — The Nestled Nook

Mielle enters the Nestled Nook hungry and uncertain about her coins. Jester notices, explicitly offers to pay for breakfast, and waits for Mielle's uncomfortable acceptance before ordering food. Mielle meets Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb, Nott, Mollymauk and Yasha. Molly and Yasha invite the room to the carnival. Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb and Nott choose to attend; Jester offers one entry for Mielle, who accepts. Mielle leaves with four coins still unvalued, a temporary shelter kept private, and a reason to return after dark.
