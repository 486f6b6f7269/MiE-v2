## C2E1 Chapter 01 — The Nestled Nook

Mielle enters the Nestled Nook hungry and uncertain about her coins. Jester notices, explicitly offers to pay for breakfast, and waits for Mielle's uncomfortable acceptance before ordering food. Mielle meets Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb, Nott, Mollymauk and Yasha. Molly and Yasha invite the room to the carnival. Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb and Nott choose to attend; Jester offers one entry for Mielle, who accepts. Mielle leaves with four coins still unvalued, a temporary shelter kept private, and a reason to return after dark.

## C2E1 Chapter 03 — The curious show

Toya sings. An old man transforms during the performance. Jester acts first with magic and a duplicate. The first creature kills a young woman before Mielle has a non-collision route through the crowd. Mielle walks into the danger lane, publicly recalls the physical bokken from Yasha's custody, uses an ordinary first strike to learn the creature's hardness, then uses black-reinforced counters while remaining unhit. The dead young woman rises as the second creature. Mielle breaks one arm and one leg of each creature, but does not kill either one. Fjord, Caleb, Nott and Molly contribute materially. Yasha kills the first creature; Beau destroys the second. Crownsguard enter, order weapons down, count the dead and forbid departure from Trostenwald. Jester stands quietly to Mielle's left afterward without demanding an explanation.
