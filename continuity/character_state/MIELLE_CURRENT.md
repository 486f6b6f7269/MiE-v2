## What she remembers

- She met Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb, Nott, Mollymauk and Yasha.
- She learned the town name Trostenwald, heard Zadash as a destination name, and learned that the Fletching and Moondrop carnival will be east of Lochward on the south bank after dark.
- Molly's carnival charges two silver. She still does not know the value of her own coins with certainty.
- Jester offered to cover one admission, and Mielle accepted. The actual payment remains for the gate.
