# C2E1-C01-NESTLED-NOOK-FIRST-CONTACT — The Nestled Nook first contact

## Objective story fact

Mielle enters the Nestled Nook hungry and uncertain whether her four unfamiliar coins can buy food. Jester notices, asks whether Mielle wants her to pay for breakfast, and orders fish stew and bread only after Mielle accepts. Mielle meets Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb, Nott, Mollymauk and Yasha. Molly and Yasha advertise their carnival; Molly reads cards for Jester and publicly invites the room. Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb and Nott each choose to attend. Mielle cannot establish that her coins cover the two-silver admission, so Jester offers to pay for one entry and Mielle accepts. Actual payment remains deferred until the carnival gate.

## Direct witnesses

- Mielle: experiences the whole scene.
- Jester, Fjord and Beau: witness Mielle's coin uncertainty, Jester's offered and accepted breakfast, Mielle's name, the visible wooden sword, the tavern conversation, Molly and Yasha's arrival, the carnival invitation, and Jester's one-entry offer.
- Caleb and Nott: meet Mielle after breakfast begins; witness her ordinary response to Nott, the shared tavern conversation, Molly and Yasha's arrival, the invitation, and Jester's one-entry offer.
- Molly and Yasha: meet the room after Mielle's breakfast; witness the carnival decision, Jester's offer to cover one entry, and Mielle's acceptance. Yasha sees Mielle's wooden sword and closed left eye in passing.
- Adelaine and incidental patrons: may observe the public food order, Mielle's red cloak and ordinary public presence, but have no basis for personal conclusions.

## What each witness can reasonably conclude

- Mielle: Jester will offer direct help without first demanding a history; Fjord and Beau are cautious but willing to speak; Caleb and Nott are guarded; Molly and Yasha work for the carnival. None of this establishes trust, private history or future allegiance.
- Jester: Mielle was hungry, does not know local coin value, gives the name Mielle, carries a wooden sword, has a closed left eye behind her left glasses lens, and accepted one carnival entry. Jester does not know why Mielle is alone, where she sleeps, why the eye is closed, or anything about her origin or abilities.
- Fjord and Beau: Mielle was uncertain with currency, carried a wooden sword, answered dryly, and said she has a place to stay “for today.” They do not know the place, reason, history, skills, or intent behind those facts.
- Caleb and Nott: Mielle introduced herself, did not make Nott's appearance into a public problem, and accepted Jester's planned carnival admission. They do not know Mielle's origin, safety, money, shelter, or reasons.
- Molly and Yasha: Mielle is a possible carnival guest whose admission Jester intends to cover. Yasha also sees the closed left eye and wooden sword as visible features. Neither knows their cause, value, or implications.

## Unsupported conclusions

- Mielle has not joined a party, accepted a debt, promised travel, or become anyone's responsibility.
- Jester's breakfast and ticket offer do not establish friendship, ownership, obligation, romance, or continuing financial support.
- No witness may infer Mielle's origin, shelter location, poverty, skill, power, history, intent, or emotional state from hunger, the coins, her cloak, the wooden sword, the closed eye, or a short answer.
- No witness knows the later carnival danger or has a reason to predict it.

## Recallable details

- Four unfamiliar coins, one cracked through the stamped face.
- Fish stew, bread, and a too-large first bite.
- Jester's charcoal-smudged hand and a winged loaf in her sketchbook.
- Nott's porcelain mask and concern with food.
- Molly's moon, crossed keys and lantern cards.
- The carnival's two-silver admission, east of Lochward on the south bank, after dark.

## Immediate consequences

- Mielle's hunger is relieved for the moment.
- Mielle has a stated reason to return after dark and keeps her temporary shelter private.
- Jester intends to cover one admission at the carnival gate; no money or ticket changes hands in the inn.
- The source group and Mielle have an evening destination without becoming a settled party.
