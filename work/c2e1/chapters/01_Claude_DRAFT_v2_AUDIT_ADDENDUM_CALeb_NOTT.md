## Resolution in v2

- Rinaldo: Jester now explains, as new information, that Rinaldo paid her, Fjord and Beau that morning for helping his daughter. Mielle objects that she did nothing to earn a share, which keeps the payment's ownership with the three of them.
- Ticket acceptance: Mielle looks at the offer a beat too long, names the breakfast already given, questions the offer, and accepts while looking at the table. The "one evening" limit is present as discomfort, not a contract; Jester lets the second half go unspoken.
- Fjord test removed: Mielle eats her stew and her nonreaction stands without praise or a verdict. Fjord's "You didn't react" line and Mielle's answer are gone.
- Caleb and Nott expanded: Caleb names himself, claims the pair's business as private, moves the salt within Nott's reach, and asks a coded question about the road east. Nott boasts about robbing the room, demands the cheese, and later blurts "I want to see the animals" before looking annoyed at having wanted anything aloud. Mielle answers only small contacts and does not soothe, solve, recruit or interpret them.
