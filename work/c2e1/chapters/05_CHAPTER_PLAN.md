## Required end state

- Crownsguard deliver Mielle, Jester, Fjord, Beau, Caleb and Nott to the Nestled Nook and leave.
- The no-leaving-Trostenwald order remains active.
- Jester asks where Mielle has actually been staying.
- Mielle reveals the abandoned building and that she has used it for two days; exact location and legal status remain unknown.
- Jester offers her room for one night.
- Mielle exposes four presumed coins; Jester identifies three coppers and one button.
- No payment occurs. All four objects remain with Mielle.
- Mielle accepts the one-night arrangement.
- No friendship, romance, debt, trust pact, party status, ownership, permanent lodging or future support is established.
- Molly remains detained with the carnival. Yasha remains on the Toya route.

## Required causal chain

1. Crownsguard escort pressure keeps the walk mostly quiet.
2. Jester makes ordinary contact; Mielle's attention and embarrassment show the private orientation already present.
3. One clumsy obstruction may create a practical cost and let Beau act physically without resetting witness knowledge.
4. Guards confirm the inn, repeat the town-level restriction and leave.
5. Jester asks the private lodging question and waits.
6. Mielle chooses bounded honesty: abandoned building, two days, no exact place.
7. At most one blunt safety beat and one official-risk beat may occur before Jester acts; neither is required if pacing works better without it.
8. Jester offers one night in her room.
9. Mielle tries to reduce obligation with the four objects.
10. Jester identifies three coppers and one button and refuses payment.
11. Nott may own one concise object reaction; Caleb may end their participation through pair-focused action.
12. Mielle's private wish for friendship makes refusal harder, but acceptance remains a bounded choice.
13. Others withdraw without an approval roll call.
14. Jester and Mielle reach the room threshold; Mielle chooses again and enters.

## Scene shape

### Scene 1 — Escorted streets

- Live condition: guard buckle, route and constrained silence.
- Jester remains close enough to become a problem for Mielle's attention.
- One physical obstruction may produce Beau's catch and a brief shared joke.
- The scene ends when the inn becomes the immediate legal destination.

### Scene 2 — Private common room

- Guards verify the inn and leave.
- Jester asks once and waits.
- Mielle reveals the abandoned building and two days.
- Beau may ask one roof/safety question.
- Fjord may state one practical consequence: the recorded address must be true tonight.
- The scene must not inventory roof, location, law, dryness, rats and food through sequential questions.
- Jester offers the room before the exchange turns into an interrogation.

### Scene 3 — Objects and threshold

- Mielle attempts contribution.
- Jester identifies the objects and refuses payment.
- Nott may respond once to the button; Caleb may redirect/withdraw with her.
- Beau and Fjord may withdraw without final commentary.
- Jester and Mielle handle ordinary room logistics.
- Mielle chooses at the threshold and the chapter ends on the physical remainder.
