# Chapter 05 planning review

## Purpose

This review rebuilds Chapter 05 planning after the prior workflow allowed repeated work-file wording to harden into apparent canon. It separates author decisions and protected results from editorial preferences before the draft is recreated.

## Authority sources

1. newest explicit user decisions recorded in `work/c2e1/chapters/05_CANON_DELTA_AMENDMENT.md`;
2. approved Chapters 01–04;
3. current continuity and relationship state;
4. stable character, setting, power and prose canon;
5. source note, Arc Map and Episode Map;
6. chapter work material;
7. templates and diagnostics.

Repetition across levels 5–7 does not increase authority.

## Root cause of the previous planning failure

The prior planning was not wholly wrong. Its problem was classification.

- Character-specific possibilities were written as participation requirements.
- Removal-test diagnostics were treated as automatic blockers for optional lines.
- Editorial targets such as one compact Caleb/Nott lane were phrased as required outcomes.
- A preferred noun or exact wording could be audited as though it were a canon fact.
- The audit compared the draft with the same over-specified planning that had generated it, producing circular validation.
- Individually good character beats accumulated into a question chain and cast roll call because each work artifact tried to prove every dossier had been applied.
