# Planning patch — Mielle's closed left eye (witness-visible fact)

This patch records the smallest planning corrections required before the Chapter 01 revision. It adds one visible feature to the witness-fact lists in the Chapter 01 plan, mini delta, and scene cards. It does not create a new Episode Canon Delta. It changes only witness-visible facts, not agency, source obligations, or outcomes.

The feature: Mielle is blind in her left eye and keeps that eye closed behind the left lens of her round glasses. It is a feature people can see, remember, or glance toward again. It is not a mystery, mood signal, power clue, trauma diagnosis, or a reason to infer anything about her history.

Under **Witness and knowledge boundaries → What may be reasonably inferred**, add to the list of visible facts:

> a closed left eye behind the left lens of her round glasses, which a person may notice and remember as an ordinary physical feature.

Under **Flexible elements**, add:

> Whether a given character notices the closed left eye, and when. Any notice is observation of a visible feature, not an inference about injury cause, history, emotion, danger, or power.
