## Evasion basis

For the Chapter 03 no-hit result, the governing basis is:

1. Mielle's exceptional martial skill: footwork, range control, balance, timing and choice of the smallest viable movement.
2. Observation Haki: short combat precognition of immediate hostile intent, line and timing before a movement fully completes.

Observation is not unlimited future sight, thought-reading, transformation-cause knowledge, immunity, teleportation or a substitute for physical space. Mielle still needs a viable route and must move around the crowd, terrain, bodies and allies.
