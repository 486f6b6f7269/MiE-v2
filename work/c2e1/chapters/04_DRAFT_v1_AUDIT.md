## Prose and POV

Pass with minor line edits.

The draft follows the new prose system better after the user's revisions. It opens on object custody, not theme. It shows legal pressure through shields, ledger, witness rows and the blocked flap. It lets Jester's cover and group silence carry relationship change without naming trust. It ends on the legal contact point pulling Mielle toward the inn where she did not sleep.

Strong retained/revised beats:

- `Jester was still on my left.` This is enough. It does not explain the care after showing it.
- Flynn's panic is separated from proof by the Watchmaster's follow-up question.
- Mielle's lodging hesitation is grounded in concrete legal risk: abandoned place, trespass, alone, incomplete answer, quill.
- The guard's nod before the bokken retrieval keeps authority visible.
- The final line leaves a concrete remainder: the written inn name leading Mielle toward a false lodging point.

Minor prose concerns:

- `the same way as theirs` should be `the same shape as theirs` or another cleaner construction.
- Jester's long cover line needs punctuation after the quote and may read better as two sentences.
- The `really really distant place` phrase is acceptable for Jester if kept as impulsive cover, but should not be allowed to become evidence that she knows anything about Mielle's origin.
