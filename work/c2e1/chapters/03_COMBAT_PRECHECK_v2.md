## Mielle capabilities used

- Soulbond recall of the same physical bokken.
- Exceptional martial skill: footwork, range control, balance, timing, parry, angle choice and counterstrikes.
- Observation Haki: short combat-prediction warning of immediate hostile intent, line and timing. It does not give unlimited future sight, thought-reading, transformation knowledge or immunity.
- Armament Haki: black coating and reinforcement on counterstrikes through the bokken after the first ordinary hit proves abnormal resistance.

## Mielle capabilities prohibited

- Advanced Haki emission or internal destruction.
- Conqueror's Haki.
- Spiral Power.
- Stand manifestation.
- Bag portal / storage solution.
- Mind-reading, emotion control, crowd control or memory manipulation.
- Healing, revival, transformation cure or transmission cure.
- Barrier, teleportation, automatic immunity or undefined defense.
- Any system name in story prose.

## No-hit rule

Every missed attack against Mielle must have a stated cause visible in the action chain. Acceptable causes include:

- Observation warning of intent, vector or timing;
- committed shoulder, planted leg, late reach or mass already spent;
- trained footwork, pivot, lean, parry or change of range;
- terrain, central pole, crowd line, blocked route or another creature's body;
- an ally's chosen action altering the creature's angle.

The no-hit result does not make the creatures harmless. They still threaten civilians, allies and route control. Mielle must still have physical room to move.

## Combat pressure waves

1. **Performance breaks:** Toya sings; old man transforms; carnival workers move Toya and begin evacuation; Jester opens with magic and duplicate.
2. **First death:** first creature attacks young woman; she evades once, is blocked by crowd, then dies. Mielle cannot reach her without creating a collision line.
3. **Mielle enters:** Mielle walks into the gap, recalls the bokken publicly, is underestimated as a small girl with a wooden sword, and draws the first creature's attention.
4. **Hardness test:** first creature commits to Mielle; she avoids by Observation-assisted footwork and strikes the planted knee without Armament. It staggers but does not break as a human would.
5. **Armament begins:** after the hardness test, Mielle uses Armament on counterstrikes. She breaks one arm of the first creature before or as weapons are returning.
6. **Second creature rises:** young woman's body becomes the second creature. Mielle keeps both in front of her and receives no hit.
7. **Group joins:** Yasha returns weapons; Fjord, Caleb, Nott and Molly take independent action. At least one of Mielle's remaining limb breaks must come from an opening their actions create.
8. **Source finishes:** Yasha kills the first creature. Beau destroys the second. Mielle does not take either finish.
9. **Official control:** Crownsguard arrive after immediate danger, order weapons down, separate witnesses, count the dead and keep involved people in Trostenwald.
