## Established fact

- Caleb and Nott enter the common room after staying upstairs; Fjord, Jester and Beau are already another party at the inn and receive payment from Rinaldo.
- Molly and Yasha arrive to advertise the Fletching and Moondrop Traveling Carnival of Curiosities. Molly invites the tavern group to that night's show.
- The group attends the carnival. During the performance, an old man and a woman transform into hostile creatures. The group fights them, both threats are stopped, and the Crownsguard keeps the carnival and the group in Trostenwald while it investigates.
