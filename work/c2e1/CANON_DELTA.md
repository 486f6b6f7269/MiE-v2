## Mechanics and power constraints

- Relevant capability or equipment: ordinary bokken; Perfect Wardrobe backpack camouflage; glasses and Bag of Holding exist but are not used.
- Stated mechanic: accessible storage cannot generate food, money, maps, medicine, tools or a safe route.
- Source gap or boundary: no named power or cognition effect solves currency, reads thoughts, produces food or creates trust. Holmes reasoning cannot turn hunger, clothing, silence, posture or one answer into a biography or motive.
- Visible consequence: food and conversation come from Jester's voluntary attention and Mielle's ordinary replies.

## Relationship and setting consequences

- A meal and an enjoyable exchange are not a relationship state, debt, promise, group membership, trust or romance.
- The chapter may establish a first workable rhythm between Jester and Mielle: Jester is curious without pity; Mielle answers a little more than necessity demands; both leave wanting to know more. This is a beginning, not an established friendship.
- Molly's later invitation remains an offer, not recruitment.
- Mielle does not obtain a complete education in prices, law, routes or custom from breakfast.
